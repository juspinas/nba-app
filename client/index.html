How to Play

Who you got?

The goal of each round is to select a stat category where your NBA player beats your opponent.

Your NBA player and their stats are shown on the left and your opponent is shown on the right (top and bottom for smaller screens). Your opponents stats are hidden and will only reveal the stat category you have chosen.

Your win/loss record is tracked at the top.

Other Info:

Inspired by Top Trumps card game

All stats are season averages from only the 2019-2020 NBA regular season

Low turnover averages beat higher turnover averages

Some player images may be incorrect or missing
